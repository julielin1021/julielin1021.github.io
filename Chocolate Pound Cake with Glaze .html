Chocolate Fudge Cookies

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour


  • 1/2 tsp salt


  • 1/2 tsp baking powder


  • 1/4 tsp baking soda


  • 1/2 cup sour cream


  • 1 tsp vanilla


  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa , (such as Fry's)


  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter , softened


  • 1 cup granulated sugar


  • 4 large eggs , beaten


    • DIRECTIONS

      2. Combine flour, salt, baking powder and soda in a bowl and stir until uniform.

      3. Combine sour cream and vanilla in a bowl and stir until uniform.

      4. Beat butter and sugar in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, and beat really well between additions.

      5. Add half of flour mixture and beat until just combined. Add sour cream mixture, and beat until incorporated. Beat in remaining flour mixture.

      6. Scrape into prepared loaf pan and bake for 60 to 65 min or until a tester comes out clean.
