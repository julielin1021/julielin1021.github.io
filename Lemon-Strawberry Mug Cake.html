Lemon-Strawberry Mug Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour


  • 3 tbsp sugar


  • 1/4 tsp baking powder


  • 1 egg


  • 2 tbsp canola oil


  • 1 tsp grated lemon zest


  • 2 tsp lemon juice


  • 1 strawberry , finely chopped


  • sliced strawberries , (optional)


  • icing sugar , (optional)


    • DIRECTIONS


      1. Spray a large microwave-safe mug with oil.

      2. Combine flour with sugar and baking powder in a small bowl.

      3. Stir in egg, oil, lemon zest and juice.

      4. Gently fold in chopped strawberries. Scrape into prepared mug.

      5. Microwave on high for 2 min. Remove and let stand for 5 min. Garnish with strawberry slices and sifted icing sugar, if desired.
